A WPI Collablab Project
Constructing a small, autonomous research vessel to cross the Atlantic.

We are a group of students from WPI who are desinging and constructing an autonomous boat to compete in the Microtransat challenge in 2020. We hope that by incorportaing new technolgies we can demonstrate the capabilities of these technologies as well as break the mictrotransat speed record.

Key Technologies


Solar Powered Drivetrain

Using a large array of solar panels and batteries the boat can maintain thrust continually and power the onboard computer and navigation. This allows for long distance travel at a useful speed without the need for fossile fuels.
